My Top 10 Favorite Dog Breeds

Samoyed Description
Samoyed is my favorite dog breed! They looks so majestic and adorable with their clean white poofy fur. They are gentle but playful at the same time.
Pomeranian Description
Pomeranians are small dogs but are super duper cute! They are really fluffy and are always willing to learn new tricks.
Alaskan Malamute Description
Alaskan Malamutes are extremely strong. They are affectionate and loyal. It's most notable feature is it's really cute face and tail.
Golden Retriever Description
Golden Retrievers are friendly and intelligent. They are a good choice for a family dog because they are very trustworthy. In addition, they're really easy to train.
Chow Chow Description
Chow Chows are known for its lion-like features. Namely its huge fluffy mane. It's very important for chow chows to exercise.
Pembroke Welsh Corgi Description
The Pembroke Welsh Corgi is a small housedog. They are fond of bonding and playing around with their owners. They are a very loving pet.
Siberian Husky Description
Siberian Huskies are athletic dogs. They are usually recognized as sled dogs due to their speed and strength.
Labrador Retriever Description
Labrador Retrievers are energetic dogs. They are known to be playful and can do many things.
German Shepherd Description
German Shepherds are trusty companions and are loyal to their owners. They are usually the main choice for police dogs.
Pug Description
Pugs are beloved dogs. They are sweet and humble. Also, they're faces look so squishy you just wanna pop!.
